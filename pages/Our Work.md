<h3>A National Framework for Safeguarding in Sport</h3>
<p>The purpose of the Safe Sport Unified Code and the Safe Sport Programme
which supports it are:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>to safeguard participants in the sporting environment from abuse and harassment
which undermine both the mental and physical health of participants and
the integrity of sport</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>to ensure harmonised and coordinated safeguarding framework across the
Singapore sporting ecosystem through organisations that are responsible
for organising, administering and facilitating participation in sport</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Together, the Safe Sport Unified Code and the Safe Sport Programme form
the national framework for safeguarding in sport.</p>
<p><strong>DID YOU KNOW?</strong>
<br>
<br><strong>Whilst guided by international frameworks on safeguarding in sport, the Safe Sport Unified Code and Safe Sport Programme has been contextualised for Singapore sporting landscape through extensive consultations with experts and sporting community stakeholders over a 9-month period.</strong>
</p>
