<h4><strong>Ensuring Safe Recruitment Processes</strong></h4>
<p>People who work or volunteer in your organisation have the responsibility
to ensure and promote a safe sporting environment for all participants,
especially Vulnerable Persons.</p>
<p>This is only possible if the people recruited are qualified and suitable.</p>
<h4><strong>Role of Organisations</strong></h4>
<p>As an organisation, your role is to ensure an effective recruitment and
selection process for staff and/or volunteers.</p>
<p>This would include:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Comprehensive application forms</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Declaration of past history and/or disciplinary proceedings</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reference checks</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Structured interviews that incorporate questions relating to suitability,
experience, values and attitudes, and best practices</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Verification of qualifications and experience</p>
</li>
</ul>
