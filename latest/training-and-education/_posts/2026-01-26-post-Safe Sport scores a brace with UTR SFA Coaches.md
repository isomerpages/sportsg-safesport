<p>As part of our continued commitment to safeguarding, a refresher Safe
Sport workshop was conducted for School Football Academy (SFA) coaches
from the Unleash the Roar (UTR) programme.&nbsp;</p>

<p>The session brought together coaches who had previously attended an earlier
version of our Safe Sport workshop back in 2022, as well as coaches who
were attending for the first time. It offered a unique opportunity for
the coaches to collaborate, revisit key principles, and strengthen best
practices in an ever-changing sporting landscape.&nbsp;</p>

in Singapore.&nbsp;</p>
