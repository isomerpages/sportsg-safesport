<p>The Safe Sport team recently conducted awareness workshops for student
leaders at <strong>Institute of Technical Education (ITE) College West, Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), and Singapore University of Social Sciences (SUSS)</strong>,
bringing together voices from across higher education to champion safer
sporting environments.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Man holding up a red card as a presenter looks at him" src="/images/ITE_West.png">
</div>
<p>The workshops taught student leaders how to identify abuse and harassment
in sport, as well as how to protect themselves and their peers. Participants
learned about how everyone is responsible in breaking cycles of abuse and
harassment which could damage the integrity of sport.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="A group of students in discussion" src="/images/SUTD_2026.png">
</div>
<p>Students were asked to reflect on their current sporting environments
and identify any potential Safe Sport risks they could observe. Through
guided discussions and case studies exploring “grey areas” of behaviour,
students unpacked complex situations and considered how best to respond.
Working through realistic scenarios helped sharpen their ability to spot
early warning signs and navigate challenging situations with confidence.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="A group of students with Safe Sport facilitators in a group photo together" src="/images/SUSS_2026.png">
</div>
<p>By educating student leaders from these three institutions, the workshops
created a network of informed advocates who can now influence their communities,
support their peers, and direct others to the right channels for help.
<br>
<br>The initiative represents an important step towards strengthening a culture
of safety across Singapore's higher education sporting landscape. If you’d

We look forward to partnering with you!</p>
