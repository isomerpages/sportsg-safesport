<h3><strong>Safeguarding Officer Training</strong></h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Trainer briefing participants for a small group role play activity" src="/images/sobanneralt.jpg">
</div>
<p>The <a href="https://www.safesport.sg/case-management/safeguarding-officer" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Safeguarding Officer</a> Training
is for appointed officers from sporting organisations in Singapore.</p>
<p>The training is a facilitated by a certified Psychological First Aid trainer
with a background in child protection and equips officers with the relevant
skills to respond to affected parties and manage disclosures of abuse.</p>
<p></p>
<h5><strong>Hybrid Format</strong></h5>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Online - self directed learning (12 hours)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Online - classroom with facilitator (0.5 day)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In-Person - classroom with facilitator (1 day)</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
<h5><strong>Topics</strong></h5>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Impact of Abuse</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Understanding Offending Behaviour</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Barriers to Reporting</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Responding to Disclosure</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reporting Procedures</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Laws on Harassment and Abuse</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>

<h5><strong>Pre Training Requirements</strong></h5>
<p>Safeguarding Officers are required to complete the Safeguarding Officer
e-learning module on <a href="https://sportsg-ed.csod.com/client/sportsg-ed/default.aspx" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">SportSG-ED</a> (different
to the Safe Sport Awareness Modules) as a pre-requisite to the classroom
training where officers will be assessed on their skills. The Safe Sport
team will grant you access to the e-learning when your registration has
been confirmed.</p>
<p>This training is chargeable for non-<a href="/safesportprogramme/memberorganisations" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">member organisations</a> of
the Safe Sport Programme.</p>
